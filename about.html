Salut ! I'm Raphaël Lewi, a multi-media artist and designer. I was born in Paris, France but I've lived in Portland, OR since 2017.

My main source of inspiration, broadly speaking, is the Esoteric. I am fascinated by archeology, ancient civilizations, mystical symbolism, and lost or bscure religious practices. I am very interested in how they relate to our modern world, how they inform and influence culture in all its aspects.

My art is not media-specific at all. I've worked on everything from sculptures to collages. I like to use both conventional and unconventional materials, and I love to blur the distinctions between mediums. At the time of writing, I am working with: paper and ink, concrete, caulk, and found toys.



I love to collaborate with other artists in my community, both as an artist and as a curator. The projects I have worked on over the years include:

BLEAK (2024)

an experimental art magazine printed in a newspaper format

The Research Room (2023)

a DIY art space covered floor-to-ceiling in aluminium foil

Scenes of the Decay (2021)

a 6-month installation project about entropy and the passage of time

Ghost Gallery (2021)

an art gallery in an abandoned building by the Columbia River, allowing artists to to exhibit their work during the pandemic

Life In Eden (2020)

my undergraduate thesis about a ruined world and a speculative religion
